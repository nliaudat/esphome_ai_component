Instructions

Goal: Mark analog clock-dials.
  1. Load Image: Click 'Load Image' to simulate your camera view.
  2. Draw Dials: Click the CENTER of the dial and drag OUTWARDS.
  3. Shape: This creates a perfect SQUARE box (Orange). Deadzone shown as cyan dashed circle.
  4. Multiple Dials: Use the Dial selector / ➕ / 🗑️ to manage them.
  5. Debug Panel: Tweak detection parameters live per dial.
  6. Output: The generated YAML contains analog_reader config block.
