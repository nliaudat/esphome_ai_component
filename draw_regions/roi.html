Instructions

Goal: Define the active area of the camera sensor.
  1. Load Image: Click 'Load Image' to simulate your camera view.
  2. Set Rotation: If your camera is mounted at an angle (e.g. 270°), use the rotation buttons in 'Camera Config'.
  3. Draw ROI: Click and drag on the image to draw a BLUE rectangle. This is the only area that will be sent to the AI.
  4. Why?: A smaller ROI (Window) acts like a digital zoom and improves frame rate/performance.
  5. Tip: Use 'Force 4:3' if you want standard aspect ratio.
  6. Apply: The board must be compiled with this ROI, or the crop zone can be set at runtime via a Home Assistant command.
